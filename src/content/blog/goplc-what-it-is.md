GoPLC is a software-defined PLC runtime written in Go. One binary. No proprietary hardware, no vendor lock-in, no programming software to install. It runs on anything from a Raspberry Pi to a datacenter server, speaks every major industrial protocol, and ships with a browser-based IDE for writing IEC 61131-3 Structured Text.
