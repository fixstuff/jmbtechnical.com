# Welcome

This is the new home for JMB Technical. Here you'll find articles on industrial automation, controls engineering, software development, and the intersection of traditional OT systems with modern IT approaches.

## What to Expect

Topics will include:

- **PLC & Controls** - Programming techniques, platform comparisons, and best practices
- **SCADA & HMI** - System design, visualization strategies, and data management
- **Industrial Protocols** - Deep dives into EtherNet/IP, Modbus, OPC UA, FINS, and more
- **Software Engineering** - Building industrial-grade software with Go, Python, and web technologies
- **AI in Automation** - Practical applications of machine learning and AI in industrial settings

Stay tuned for more content.
